# Exercise - Operator dashboard design

## Goal

Design a dashboard around operational questions rather than available data.

## Instructions

Choose one service and define:

1. Primary audience.
2. Top five questions the dashboard must answer.
3. Panels required to answer those questions.
4. Panels visible without scrolling.
5. Drilldown links to traces, logs or dependency views.
6. One panel you intentionally exclude.

## Expected outcome

A strong answer prioritizes health, traffic, errors and latency near the top, groups related panels and creates a clear path from symptom to evidence.

## Instructor notes

Ask learners who will use the dashboard during an incident. If the audience is unclear, the dashboard will likely become crowded and unfocused.
